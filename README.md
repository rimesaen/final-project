## Objectives

> This project aims to produce a solution for civil infrastructure inspection, specifically by detecting and mapping cracks on concrete walls. Such an objective will be achieved through the usage of an autonomous drone swarm, where each drone is equipped with a camera which will allow them to obtain data for 1) image analysis and 2) photogrammetry. In this specific case, autonomous refers to the capability to complete their mission without recurring human intervention; the system will be centralized.

> The swarm will be capable of mapping out a fully enclosed interior infrastructure. Due to time and budget constraints, the ideal outcome will not be a complete end-to-end product but will be the successful execution of the following scenario:
> The environment will be a simple room without furniture, where a wall with cracks on it will be situated. The flight path for the swarm will be autonomously plotted as it explores, and the drones will follow this flight path while periodically capturing images. The images will then be combined and processed to 1) create a 2D rendering of the complete test environment and 2) detect cracks on each surface of the test environment.
